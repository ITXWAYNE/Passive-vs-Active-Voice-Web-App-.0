Active vs Passive Voice

Welcome to Active and Passive Voice Web app

This platform is designed for teachers and anyone who teaches English Here, you’ll find valuable resources in Languages

Our Purpose

The purpose of this web app is to teach and guide users in understanding the difference between active and passive voice in English grammar. It provides clear explanations, examples, practice exercises, and a video lesson to help learners recognize, form, and use both voices effectively in writing and speech.

What This Web App is About

  • Definition of Voice – Explains how verbs relate subjects and actions.
  • Active vs Passive Voice – Shows the difference and provides clear examples.
  • When & Why to Use Passive Voice – Helps users understand contexts for passive construction.
  • Forming Passive Sentences – Step-by-step instructions and formulas for conversion.
  • Recognition of Passive Voice – Tips for identifying passive constructions in text.
  • Practice & Reflection – Exercises for users to apply what they learn.
  • Video Lesson Integration – Provides visual and auditory guidance for enhanced learning.
